Difference between <strong> and <b>

Difference between <strong> and <b> Tags

<strong> is used to indicate important or emphasized text. It gives semantic meaning and helps with accessibility tools like screen readers.

<b> is used only to make text bold visually without adding any extra importance or meaning.

When to Use:

  • Use <strong> when you want to highlight important content for emphasis or accessibility.
  • Use <b> when you just want to make the text appear bold for styling purposes.
